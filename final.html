Abstract

SELFI3-D is a project focused on creating high-quality 3D face reconstructions from various views of a person, as well as applying custom texture mapping to the resulting geometry. Our goal is to develop a pipeline that can generate accurate 3D face models and enable creative applications such as virtual face tattoos. After testing different 3D reconstruction methods, we have selected DUSt3R as our primary reconstruction method due to its superior performance with uncalibrated image pairs.

Technical Approach

Our pipeline is broken down into three main steps: 1) Reconstruction, 2) Texture Mapping, 3) Custom Application.
